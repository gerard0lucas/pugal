About Pugalidam Constructions

Your trusted construction partner with over 14 years of experience

Building Excellence Since 2010

Pugalidam Constructions is a leading construction company committed to delivering excellence in every project we undertake. With years of experience and a dedicated team of professionals, we specialize in residential, commercial, and infrastructure development.

Our company is built on the foundation of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We pride ourselves on using modern construction techniques, premium materials, and sustainable practices to create structures that stand the test of time.

From concept to completion, we ensure every project meets the highest standards of safety and craftsmanship. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in the construction industry.
